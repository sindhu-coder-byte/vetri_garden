About Us

Who We Are

Founded in 2025, Vetri Garden was created to make plant care easy, beautiful, and accessible for everyone—from first-time plant parents to lifelong gardeners. Our mission is simple: bring greenery into every home with curated plants, expert care tips, and a smooth online experience. At Vetri Garden, we’re not just a plant store—we’re your plant partner.

What We Offer

  • Hand-selected indoor and outdoor plants
  • Eco-friendly packaging with safe doorstep delivery
  • Detailed care guides for each plant
  • A natural and soothing shopping experience

Our beginner-friendly bundles are designed to help new plant lovers start with confidence. Every plant comes with care instructions so it stays healthy and happy. We provide safe, eco-conscious packaging and quick delivery to your doorstep.
