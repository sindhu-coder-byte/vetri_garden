About Us

Who We Are

Founded in 2025, Vetri Garden was created to make plant care easy, beautiful, and accessible for everyone from first-time plant parents to lifelong gardeners. Our mission is to bring greenery into every home with thoughtfully selected plants, helpful care tips, and a seamless online experience. At Vetri Garden, we're more than just a plant store.

What We Offer

  • Healthy, hand-selected indoor and outdoor plants
  • Safe, eco-friendly packaging and doorstep delivery
  • Easy-to-follow care guides for every plant
  • A nature-inspired shopping experience for every lifestyle

Our best-selling plant bundles make it easy for beginners to get started with confidence. Each plant comes with easy-to-follow care tips, so you always know how to help it thrive. We offer doorstep delivery across cities with safe and eco-friendly packaging.
