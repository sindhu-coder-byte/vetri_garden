Plant Care

How to Water Your Plants

Watering plants may seem simple, but doing it correctly is essential to keeping them healthy and thriving. The first step is to check the soilâ€”stick your finger one to two inches deep. If it's still moist, wait before watering again. This helps avoid overwatering, a common cause of plant stress and root rot.

The Best Time

Early morning is ideal for watering as plants absorb moisture before the sun heats the soil. Evening watering keeps soil damp too long, encouraging pests and fungi. Always water at the base of the plant rather than the leaves to avoid mold and disease.
